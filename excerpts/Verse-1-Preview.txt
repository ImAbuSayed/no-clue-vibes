Sard hawaavan ch udey tera silk gown,
Main kalla ghumda haan crossing the town.
Teri nakhre di garmi kare burn the floor,
Main nede aawan mangda haan little more.
Pichli mulakaat da nasha still deep,
Main jaagda haan raatan no time to sleep.
Teri akh di ishaarat wangu secret code,
Main labhda haan manzil on a dark road.
