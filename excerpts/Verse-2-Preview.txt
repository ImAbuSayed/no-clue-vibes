Dhundli fiza ch ud-da dark smoke,
Main kalla nikalda haan without a joke.
Tere ishq ch peeti main bitter truth,
Main darda nai jagg ton showing my youth.
Wafadaari da khun dulda blood and sweat,
Main karda haan poora no regret.
Ik dusre te vaarde fire and ice,
Main sab kujh haarda haan pay the price.
